Wilbee Films is a video production company made up of Scott Wilson, Rosalind Beeson and Ross Wilson.

Based in sunny Bristol, UK, we offer full production and post-production services, with particular interest in everything comedy: from live shows, sketches, shorts, sitcom pilots – we are always looking for our next collaboration with actors, writers, producers and crew.

Scott

Scott has worked all over the shop as a Video Editor, Script Coordinator and Production Assistant as a freelancer in TV and corporate video. He's also head of sketches for Up the Antics, a comedy troupe based in Bristol, helping produce their stage shows, videos and podcast.

Find him on Hiive, IMDb, and Twitter

Ros

Ros has been working in the TV and Film industry racking up Production Assistant, 3rd AD and Art Department credits like achievements in a video game. Outside of work ever focused on her quest, to write and produce awesome comedy, she has plays, shorts, sketches and writing credits galore to her name.

Ross
